About Us

Who We Are

At Red Card, we believe every celebration deserves something truly special.
Since our journey began, our mission has been to design greeting cards
that don’t just carry words — but emotions, memories, and love.

From birthdays to anniversaries, our premium quality cards are crafted to make
moments unforgettable. With a passionate team and innovative designs,
we’re here to bring a smile to your face.




Why We're Here

We are passionate about what we do. We work hard every day to make the world a more thoughtful and caring place. Together with our members, we share
conviction that nurturing relationships and making a difference
people lives the most important we can spend our time.




What We Believe

We believe that wishes are granted, dreams come true and anything is possible. We know that not every day is a holiday, but every day is a good day and so we celebrate, comfort and commemorate.
